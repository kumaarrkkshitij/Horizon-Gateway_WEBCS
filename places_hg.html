Explore Dubai's Iconic Marvels

Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa, standing at an astonishing 828 meters, is the tallest building in the world and a symbol of Dubai’s innovation and ambition. This architectural masterpiece offers breathtaking views from its observation decks, At the Top, on the 124th and 148th floors. Visitors can marvel at the city’s skyline, the Arabian Gulf, and the desert beyond. Whether you're exploring its luxurious interiors, dining in its elegant restaurants, or simply admiring its iconic silhouette, the Burj Khalifa is an unforgettable experience that showcases Dubai's modern grandeur.

Entrance Fee: Starting from 359 AED.

TopGolf Dubai

Topgolf Dubai combines high-tech golfing with a lively social scene. Featuring climate-controlled bays and interactive games for all skill levels, it also offers a vibrant restaurant and bar. Ideal for casual outings, corporate events, or family fun, it's a top destination for entertainment in the city.

Entrance Fee: Ranges from 140 to 250 AED.

The Dubai Mall

The Dubai Mall, the world's largest mall by total area, spans over 1.1 million square meters. It features over 1,200 stores, an indoor aquarium, an ice rink, and a variety of dining options. More than just a shopping center, it’s a premier destination offering endless entertainment and luxury.

The Dubai Fountain

The Dubai Fountain, the world’s largest choreographed fountain system, spans 275 meters and features water jets that reach up to 150 meters high. Set against the Burj Khalifa and Dubai Mall, it offers mesmerizing water, light, and music shows. Fountain shows occur daily at various times, with the schedule provided below.

Entrance Fee: Ranges from 140 to 250 AED.

At.mosphere at Burj Khalifa

The Atmosphere Restaurant, situated on the 122nd floor of the Burj Khalifa, offers stunning panoramic views of Dubai. With its elegant design and exceptional international cuisine, it provides a luxurious dining experience high above the city.

Skydive Dubai

Skydive Dubai offers an exhilarating experience with breathtaking aerial views of Dubai's iconic landmarks and landscapes, providing an unforgettable freefall adventure.

Entrance Fee: Ranges from 1199 to 2199 AED.
